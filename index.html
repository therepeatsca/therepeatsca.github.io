ABOUT

The Repeats play the biggest indie and rock hits that keep the dance floor filled from the first note until the final encore. Their show consists of the high octane anthems that will have your guests on their feet and the perfectly timed slow ballads for when they need a break or want that slow dance moment. The Repeats ensure that you and your guests get the best party band around. Ranging from Foo Fighters and Queens Of The Stone Age, to The Knack and Fleetwood Man, rocked up versions of classics such as Stevie Wonder's “Superstition” to a faithful recreation indie and alternative greats like Jimmy Eat World's “The Middle” The Repeats know how to get everyone on the dance floor and keep them there for the night.

"The Repeats put on a great show and had the dance floor packed. Really impressed with them and can't wait to have them back soon. Professional too!"
- Lonely Hearts Club
